## Condition #11 analysis (breaking change on verdict shape)

`wdl-contract.yaml` does NOT have a `recommendation: ask_user` field in the
verdict schema. Adding one would be a **breaking change** to the verdict shape
(`workflows/wdl-contract.yaml` §8 versioning: "Breaking changes to verdict
shape, gate sub-criteria, or exemption allowlist require a new LACOUNCIL
proposal (supermaioria strategy)."). The current proposal was approved with
strategy `maioria`, not `supermaioria`.

**Decision:** do NOT add `recommendation` to the verdict shape. The ladder is
enforced at the orchestrator + preflight layer (orchestrator instruments
`log_user_question()` before every `ask_user`; preflight validates the log
sequence). The verdict stays tri-state (`READY | DEFER | ESCALATE`); DEFER's
`block_reason` is the right surface for "ladder exhausted". This satisfies
condition #11 without supermaioria.

| `cluster_id` slug collision (subagentes usam o mesmo slug para semânticas diferentes) | `cluster_id` validado por regex `^[a-z][a-z0-9-]{2,80}$` + comentários no schema orientando os orchestrators a usar slugs estáveis |

| Preflight sub-check gera ruído para meta-projetos | Sub-check é advisory-only (não blocking) para tier M0 e meta-audit skips |
